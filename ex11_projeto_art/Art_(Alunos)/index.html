Fiat Marea

Fabricado entre 1996 e 2008

Marea

The Fiat Marea is a small family car available as a saloon and an estate, produced by the Italian automaker Fiat. Launched in September 1996, the Marea models were essentially different body styles of Fiat's hatchback offerings, the Bravo and Brava.

lamborghini diablo

lamborghini Diablo

The Lamborghini Diablo (meaning "devil" in Spanish), is a series of high-performance V12, rear mid-engined sports cars in the supercar market segment, built by Italian automobile manufacturer Lamborghini from 1990 through 2001. It is the first production Lamborghini with a top speed in excess of 200 mph (322 km/h).

Chevrolet Caravan

Fabricado entre 1974 a 1992

Caravan

Sob o capô, a Caravan 1976 possuía um motor 2.5L de 4 cilindros, que gera 98 cv e 57,9 kgfm de torque. Essa motorização ia de 0 a 100 km/h em 17 segundos, com uma velocidade máxima de 150 km/h, oferecendo um desempenho modesto, mas satisfatório.
